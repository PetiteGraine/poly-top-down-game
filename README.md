# **Spell or Mace**

![spell-or-mace](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bddd298a-0dfb-4323-b836-e65370932325)

Navigate through a dangerous dungeon, hunt for upgrades, and defeat the Turtle King in this Isaac-inspired top-down action game.

## **The Objective**

Clear multiple rooms to gather powerful augments and choose your favorite weapon. Once you are prepared, face the Boss: a powerful entity that summons slimes, teleports when hit, and can swap your position with its projectiles.

## **Equipment & Augments**

You can carry **one weapon** and equip up to **three augments** simultaneously.

**Weapons:**

* **Mace:** High damage, slow attack speed.
* **Axe:** Balanced damage and speed.
* **Frost Staff:** Ranged, very fast, low damage.
* **Fire Staff:** Ranged, moderate damage, moderate speed.

**Augments:**

* **Gloves:** Increases attack speed.
* **Armor:** Increases health.
* **Boots:** Increases movement speed.
* **Ring:** Increases damage.

## **How to play**

* **WASD / Arrow Keys:** Move the character.
* **Left Click:** Attack.
* **Right CLick(Hold):** Change camera angles.
* **E:** Interact.
